Welcome to MotoAdventure Club

Start your engines and kickstart your weekends with MotoAdventure Club - the premier monthly breakfast run club for motorcycle enthusiasts. For just € 20 a month, you'll join a community of riders who share your passion for two-wheeled adventures and hearty breakfasts

Here's what you can expect:

  • Monthly guided rides to handpicked breakfast spots
  • Scenic routes that showcase the best of our local landscapes
  • A mix of road types to suit all riding preferences
  • Opportunity to connect with fellow riders and make new friends
  • Exclusive member discounts at partner cafes and gear shops

Our rides typically take place on the first Saturday of each month, departing at dawn to catch those perfect morning roads. After a couple of hours of invigorating riding, we'll stop at a cozy cafe or diner to refuel with a delicious breakfast and lively conversation.

Whether you ride a cruiser, sport bike, tourer, or anything in between, MotoAdventure Club welcomes all makes and models. It's not about what you ride, but the joy of riding together.

Join us for your monthly dose of motorcycling, morning air, and mouthwatering breakfasts. Sign up now and let's hit the road!

What does your subscription cover?

Your subscription allows us to cover our administration fees, employ the guide and sweepers and provide one saftety support vehicle. We also make use of the subscription fees to pay an initial deposit at the restaurants, pubs or coffee shops we stop in at along the way so we can book tables ahead of time.

Safety First

At MotoAdventure Club, your safety is our top priority. We believe that a great ride is a safe ride. Here are our key safety principles and requirements:

  1. Gear Up: All riders must wear full protective gear (see below.)
  2. Bike Check: Ensure your motorcycle is in good working condition before each ride. We'll conduct brief pre-ride checks focusing on tires, brakes, lights, and fluid levels.
  3. Group Riding Etiquette: We'll review group riding guidelines before each outing, including hand signals, staggered formation, and safe following distances
  4. Ride Your Own Ride: While we ride as a group, each rider is responsible for their own safety. Never push beyond your comfort level or skill set.
  5. Zero Tolerance for Alcohol/Drugs: Any rider suspected of being under the influence will not be allowed to participate.
  6. First Aid: Our ride leaders are trained in basic first aid and carry first aid kits.
  7. Emergency Contacts: We keep a list of emergency contacts for all members. Please ensure your information is up to date.
  8. Weather Considerations: Rides may be postponed or cancelled in case of severe weather conditions.
  9. Insurance: All riders must have valid motorcycle insurance as required by law.
  10. Rider Skill Levels: We organize rides suitable for various skill levels. Always choose rides appropriate for your experience.

We carry puncure patch and plug kits, as well as tubes for most popular tyre sizes. We also contract with Nationwide Motorcycle Recovery in the event of a situation arising such as a breakdown or a puncture that we cannot repair on the road.

Remember, safety is a shared responsibility. By joining MotoAdventure Club, you agree to adhere to these safety guidelines and to ride responsibly at all times.

Essential Equipment

To ensure a minimum level of safety and comfort during the breakfast run we insist that the following equipment be used by all riders and pillions:

  1. Helmet
  2. Riding Jacket
  3. Riding pants
  4. Motorcycle Gloves
  5. Motorcycle Boots
